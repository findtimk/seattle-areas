Methodology

Each neighborhood is scored 0–100 on six dimensions: budget headroom, walkability, schools, transit, quietness, and commute. Your weights set how much each counts. Fit score is the weighted average — meaningful only relative to other neighborhoods on this page.

"What your budget buys" = budget ÷ local $/sqft. Directionally useful, not a guarantee — actual listings vary by lot, condition, vintage, and view. Commute estimates assume typical AM drive to South Lake Union.

Per-field source attribution lives in the neighborhood data file. Schools field is the most uncertain — verify per specific address before relying on it.
